Project Pixel

Turn Based - JRPG

A HD-2D Style Turn-based JRPG built in Unreal Engine 5 - a narrative asventure where each turn requires strategy and anticipation for elemental attacks and reactions.

NOTE: The characters sprites are taking from various websites. The only 2D Sprite I made was the amazing combat dummy

Combat Design Encounter Design
Turn-Based Combat
Original Final Fantasy-inspired turn order driven by Speed attribute. Player enters combat level, faces dynamic enemies, and leverages Accuracy, Evasion, and Damage investments to triumph.
Turn Order Speed Stat Combat Level Dynamic Enemies
"Every turn is a choice; every stat a strategy." Load Combat
Elemental Design
Elemental Combat
Nine Elemental types each with their own strengths and weaknesses. Harness them all as you progress through the adventure and play them to your strengths.
Gameplay Ability System Elemental Effects 9 Elements
"Elements as tools; tactics as expressions of will." Load Elements
Progression Design
Attribute & Skill Points
Invest skill points into Speed, Accuracy, Evasion, and Damage. Health scales with player level. Every point spent becomes a declaration of playstyle-whether you favor precision strikes, nimble dodging, or overwhelming force.
Accuracy Speed Evasion Damage Skill Points
"Progression as identity-become the fighter you choose to be." Load Progression
Quest Architecture
Dynamic Quest & NPC System
Quests spawn and reposition NPCs across the world. Ambient NPCs trigger contextual dialogues tied to active and completed quests. The world responds to your progress.
NPC Spawning Dynamic Positioning Dialogue Triggers Dialogue Trees
"Quests as living systems; NPCs as witnesses to your journey." Load Quests
